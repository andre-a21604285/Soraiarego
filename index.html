 About ME

I started my movie carrer in 2006, working as a production assistant. I finished my studies at portuguese Film School (Escola Superior de Teatro e Cinema) in 2009, specializing in photography.

In 2010, while working as a trainee at the rental company "Planar",  I worked as video operator until 2014,  when I became a 2nd AC. Since then I have worked in both fiction and commercials,  national and international productions of different sizes, budgets, genres , logistics and crews, not only in Portugal but also in countries like Spain, France, Canada, Cabo Verde, Vietnam, Japan... 

Since 2018 I have been working as a 1st AC, with all sorts of Cameras (Arri, Red, Blackmagic, Sony...) and lenses in both digital and Film (16mm and 35mm).

I am based in Portugal and besides my native Portuguese language, I speak fluent English and a bit of Spanish.
